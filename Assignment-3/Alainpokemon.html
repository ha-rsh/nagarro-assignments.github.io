Mega Charizard X

Mega Charizard X is a large, dragon-like Pokémon that differs greatly from its pre-evolved form. The red skin coloration of Charmeleon is no longer apparent, as Charizard appears to have reverted to the orange/yellow coloration of Charmander. Two large fangs can be seen when its mouth is closed.

Tyranitar

Tyranitar is a large, bipedal dinosaurian Pokémon. It has a gray-blue diamond-shaped scale on its belly and has a hard green-colored armor covering its entire body, with some holes on it. Several spikes protrude from its body. It has a pair of sharp, pointed teeth on both of its jaws, along with a large powerful tail protruding from its back.

Bisharp

Bisharp is a tall humanoid-looking warrior-like Pokémon. Its body is heavily armored and appears to be made of metal. Like its pre-evolution, Pawniard, Bisharp has a helmet-like red and gray dome on its forehead with a large and very sharp double-edged yellow blade protruding from its forehead and extending down the back of its head.

Metagross

Metagross resembles a robotic spider. It has a light blue disc-shaped body with two red eyes and a large X in the center of its face. Each of its four legs is outfitted with three short claws. Concept artwork of Mega Metagross. Mega Metagross is a result of one Metagross, one Metang and two Beldum linked up.

Unfezant

Unfezant is a large avian Pokémon that is based on a pheasant. Unfezant differ in appearance depending on their gender; male Unfezant are very colorful with a green belly and pink ribbon-like feathers protruding from their heads, but female Unfezant are colored brown and have slightly higher speed stats due to their lighter plumage.
